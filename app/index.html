How It Works

  • Step 1

    Login using google or facebook accounts.
    Add accounts for your children.

  • Step 2

    Go to Add rewards page.
    Set goals and rewards using the Add rewards feature.

  • Step 3

    Start by Adding assessments to your child`s todo list.
    Select from more then 40 topics -> Subtopics

  • Step 4

    Receive email notifications once the assessments are taken.
    Approve to assessments to award points to child.

  • Step 5

    Child orders rewards after earning sufficient points
    and you hand over the rewards to child.
